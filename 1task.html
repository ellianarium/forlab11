Everyday make-up: tips


Light makeup is perfect for work, walking with friends and dating during the day. At the same time, it is important to choose the correct and fashionable makeup for every day. Wondering how to do simple and beautiful makeup? Here are general guidelines and tips for creating a stylish look:

  1. Use a minimum of makeup.

  2. Do not use saturated lipstick colors, too dark shadows, glitters and other attributes of evening makeup.

  3. If you want to emphasize your eyes with bright shadows, pencil, arrows, then choose natural shades of lipstick or gloss for your lips. Highlighting the eyes and lips looks gross and cheap at the same time, especially in a casual look.

  4. Naturalness and naturalness, as well as minimalism and brevity are in fashion. Leading fashion stylists and makeup artists recommend doing simple eye makeup using natural, light and pastel shades of eyeshadow.

What do you need for everyday makeup? You don't need to put a lot of makeup on your face to look natural and natural. Main rules:

  • tidy up the skin;
  • make your look more open and expressive;
  • add shine and volume to your lips.

These are the tips from makeup artist Jeanine Lobell, who created Cate Blanchett's makeup for the 2016 Oscars, and Meryl Streep's character in The Devil Loves Prada.

Jeanine Lobell

To do this, use a minimum of cosmetics. For everyday daytime makeup, the following tools are useful:

  • Light foundation or mineral powder. Mineral-based powder is non-irritating, suitable for sensitive and problem skin. High-quality cream and powder not only masks skin imperfections, but also promotes their healing.

  • Primer. Light everyday makeup is not complete without this cosmetic mineral product. It will give the skin a matte finish, even out the tone, and make the face radiant.

  • Concealer. The modern beauty market offers concealers, which contain substances that can lighten age spots, dry small pimples. These products are often fortified with skin-friendly vitamins and antioxidants.

  • Blush. According to the experts of the fashion magazine Elle, in everyday makeup, ruddy cheeks look organic against the background of porcelain skin. Choose a blush that matches your lipstick.

    Make-up for every day step by step

    Every year, fashion trends in makeup change partially or radically. Most recently, girls were fond of face contouring, growing or painting thick eyebrows, painting their lips with matte lipsticks of various shades. These beauty tricks are no longer recommended for use in daytime light makeup.
    Cosmopolitan called the most relevant minimalistic nude makeup, which suits all types of appearance and is appropriate at any age.

    How to make stylish everyday makeup step by step? Follow the instructions:

    1. Cleanse your skin of impurities, makeup, sweat and more. To do this, use a special wash gel, micellar water or a cleansing toner.
    2. Prepare your skin for cosmetic applications. To do this, use a moisturizer. To better absorb the cream and invigorate the skin, apply the cream using the Asian technique - with light pats.
    3. After the cream has been absorbed, go about applying a foundation. Use a primer to even out your skin tone. If there is redness, pimples and other blemishes, use a concealer.
    4. Apply some blush to the cheekbones with a wide brush and blend them towards the temples.
    5. Accentuate the eyes. Choose a light makeup without arrows. For a deeper look, apply a beige, silvery cream, or champagne shade to the lids. With brown shadows, bring the lower eyelid along the lash line. Use black or brown mascara for a wide-eyed look.
    6. Shine your lips. An ideal choice is a glossy lip gloss in pink, peach, beige.

    Nude makeup is natural and invisible. To give the skin a radiance, experts recommend using tinting agents with the addition of microscopic sparkling particles.
